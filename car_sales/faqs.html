Frequently Asked Questions

How can I rent a car?

To rent a car, simply visit our website and navigate to the "Rent Out" section. There, you can browse our available cars, select the desired dates, and make a reservation. If you need any assistance, feel free to contact our support team.

What types of payment do you accept?

We accept various forms of payment, including credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, and online payment platforms such as PayPal. You can choose the most convenient payment method during the checkout process.

Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your reservation by contacting our support team or accessing your account on our website. Please note that there may be certain restrictions and fees associated with modifications or cancellations, depending on the specific terms and conditions of your reservation.

Do you offer insurance for rented cars?

Yes, we offer insurance options for rented cars. During the reservation process, you will have the opportunity to select the desired insurance coverage based on your needs and preferences. Our team can also provide more information and guidance regarding insurance options.
