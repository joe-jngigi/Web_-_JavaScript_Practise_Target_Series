Terms of Service and Rental Agreements

1. Rental Period

The rental period begins at the scheduled pick-up time and ends at the scheduled drop-off time. Any extension or modification of the rental period must be agreed upon in advance.

2. Reservation and Payment

A valid reservation requires a deposit or full payment, as specified. Payment can be made through our online payment system or in person at the rental location. Payments are non-refundable unless otherwise stated.

3. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made within 48 hours of the scheduled pick-up time are subject to a cancellation fee. Cancellations made prior to 48 hours may be eligible for a partial or full refund, depending on the circumstances.

4. Driver's License and Age Restrictions

All drivers must possess a valid driver's license and meet the minimum age requirements specified by the rental company. Additional fees may apply for drivers under the age of 25.

5. Vehicle Usage and Restrictions

The rented vehicle should be used for personal transportation purposes only and should not be used for illegal activities, racing, off-roading, or towing. Additional restrictions may apply.

6. Insurance Coverage

Our rental vehicles come with basic insurance coverage. Additional coverage options are available at an extra cost. The renter is responsible for any damages or loss not covered by insurance.

7. Fuel Policy

The rental vehicle should be returned with the same fuel level as it had at the start of the rental. A refueling fee will apply if the vehicle is returned with less fuel.

8. Maintenance and Repairs

The renter is responsible for regular maintenance such as oil changes and tire rotations. Any necessary repairs resulting from negligence or improper use will be the responsibility of the renter.

9. Return Condition

The rental vehicle should be returned in the same condition as it was rented, clean and free from damage. Additional cleaning or repair fees may apply if the vehicle is returned in poor condition.

10. Governing Law

These terms and conditions are governed by the laws of the jurisdiction in which the rental takes place. Any disputes arising from the rental agreement will be resolved in accordance with the applicable laws.
